Investing in Communities

Supporting People and Communities around the World

Our Commitment

We’re committed to being a good corporate citizen, helping to improve society and quality of life where we operate around the world. We work to protect the health, safety and security of our employees and the public, responsibly manage our socioeconomic impacts and uphold respect for human rights in all of our operations.

We strive to collaborate with all stakeholders, including suppliers, customers and our workforce, to help enhance the benefits of our business to local communities. Our investments in local communities play a key role in meeting our commitments.

Supporting Communities

Local investments, focused on economic and educational benefits, are one of the key ways we support communities in which we operate. We work closely with local communities to understand their unique needs, identify and help contribute to long-term, positive local economic and social development, and support effective risk management.
